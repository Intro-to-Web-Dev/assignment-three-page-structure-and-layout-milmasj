Who?

I am a student currently attending my first year at Dunwoody College of Technology studying graphic design in Minneapolis, MN. Fashion, photography, fine art, design, comedy, and many more, fuel my bold, dramatic yet clean style.​

Inspiration

As a (graphic) designer, I spend hours in front of my computer. This is voluntary as I am fueled by curiosity ​and inspired by others, culture around me, and a constantly changing world around me. I am frequently asking myself, "how could this be improved or changed?"—All of my work is done with intention.

Accomplished

My curiosity and determination have lead me to be a part of countless creative project endeavors. Most recently i've created an interview based magazine named Future Intern. With a focus on design and my love for fashion in the past inspired me to be a part of creating a clothing company in high school. It didnt stop there. I was a part of the 2020 Nordstrom Fashion ambassador’s Program. My ability to multitask while still keeping a clear vision and strong grip on whats important lead me to graduating Jefferson High School with the class of 2020.
